About Us

The Museum of Online Art was established to create a space for everyone to access art that is within the public domain. Many of these pieces have been forgotten or lost. It is our goal to help educate art lovers on the history of the art and the artist.


The Museum was started by Ian Ashby in his Wed Fundamentals course at BYU Idaho in 2021. Ian chose this topic and theme because of his love for art. He believes that our cultures are important to celebrate and remember.


Ian would love to hear from your, so please contact us here!
